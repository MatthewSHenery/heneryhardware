About Henery Hardware

Thank you for choosing Henery Hardware

Henery Hardware is a locally-owned hardware store with four locations including its newest store in Anacortes: Sebo's Henery Hardware.

In every market we serve, we are recognized as having knowledgeable and friendly staff to help you with whatever project that may have brought you into our store.

We offer a broad range of services including key making and Chip keys, color matching for paint projects, and a rental section with much to choose from.

Please shop online for FREE shipping directly to your nearest store.
